Thai Street Food by Arissara

A Taste of Thailand in Southern New Hampshire.

Chef Arissara Krakow grew up in central Thailand, where she learned cooking at home from her extended family. Upon moving to Peterborough, she began cooking for booths at farmers markets and other public events, before aquiring her truck. Since 2020, Total Thai Food Truck has been offering street food typical of Thailand in the Monadnock region and throughout much of southern New Hampshire. In addition to the food truck, Arissara also offers catering, special orders and Thai cooking classes.
